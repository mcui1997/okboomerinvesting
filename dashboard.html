Employer Retirement Accounts (401k, HSA, 403b)

What you need to find in YOUR plan:

  • S&P 500 or Total Stock Market Fund - Look for fund names like "S&P 500 Index", "500 Index Fund", "Vanguard 500"
  • International Fund - Look for "International Equity", "EAFE Index", "Developed Markets"
  • Small Cap Fund - Look for "Small Cap Index", "Small/Mid Cap Index", "Extended Market"
  • Money Market or Stable Value Fund - Look for "Money Market", "Stable Value", "Cash Equivalent"

How to use these charts: The tickers shown below (SPY, VXUS, VB) are REFERENCE charts. Check their RSI readings, then apply those same valuation zones to the similar funds in YOUR employer plan.
